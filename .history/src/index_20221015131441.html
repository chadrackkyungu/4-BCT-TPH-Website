About Us

The Teenpreneur Hub

The Teenpreneur Hub is a teenage-based organisation with students that are built by the future. We aim to impact lives while making money. We do this through skills based learning in the disciplines of Technology, where we learn to create websites such as this one that was created by the students of the Technology Hub, Media where we learn about photography and produce quality photos such as the ones posted on this webpage as well as Arts where we learn to do Graphic Designing of Business Logos such as our Teenpreneur Hub logo.

Q-Leds

Q-Leds is a teenage-based business that was started by the students of the TeenPreneur Hub as part of their Entrepreneurial learning. Q-Leds is a project that was started to solve the problem of load shedding that is currently plaguing the country. Q-Leds is a light system that helps young people overcome the current load shedding problem by giving them a source of light to study at night. It also features a charging port and speakers as well as a holographic feature which is our unique selling feature of the Q-Leds lighting system. It is built by the youth for the youth.
